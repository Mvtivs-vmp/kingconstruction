What Do We Do?

Custom Masonry: From the construction of complex structures to the finest detail in masonry, we tailor our services to your specific needs.

Steps: Design and execution of steps that combine functionality and aesthetics.

Retaining Walls: We build durable and resistant walls to guarantee the stability of your land.

Concrete Sidewalks: Creation of high-quality concrete sidewalks that ensure durability and resistance.

Concrete Driveways: We design driveways that stand the test of time and constant traffic.

Stone Veneer: We apply stone veneers for an elegant and durable appearance.

Belgian Blocks: Professional installation of Belgian blocks for a distinctive and robust finish.

Fireplaces: Construction and remodeling of fireplaces that bring warmth and style to your home.

Basement Waterproofing: Protect your basement from moisture with our effective waterproofing solutions.

Exterior French Drainage: We install French drainage systems to avoid water accumulation problems in your property.

At King Construction, every project is an opportunity to demonstrate our dedication and skills. From first contact to completion, our team works with you to ensure that every detail is perfect. Your satisfaction is our priority and our experience is your guarantee.
